Harihar fort


Harihar Fort, also known as Harshagad, is a historic hill fort located in the Nashik district of Maharashtra, India.It stands at an altitude of 3,676 feet (1,120 meters). The fort is built on a triangular prism of rock, making its structure unique. It's renowned for its unique rock-cut steps, which are almost 80 degrees vertical and lead to the top of the fort.
